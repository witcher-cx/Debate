About the Club

Founded on July 16, 1996, the Motijheel Government Boys' School Debating Club has been at the forefront of nurturing Bangladesh's brightest debating minds for nearly three decades.

We've successfully organized 18 national debate competitions, creating a platform where critical thinking meets eloquent expression. Our club has become a breeding ground for future leaders, lawyers, journalists, and changemakers.
